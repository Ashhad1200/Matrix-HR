## 📄 MatrixHR — Project Brief

**Product Name:** MatrixHR
**Tagline:** The connected HR platform built for South Asia and the Middle East.
**Type:** Multi-tenant SaaS — HR & Payroll Software

---

### 🎯 The Opportunity

Global HR platforms like BambooHR don't serve our region well:
- No Pakistani payroll (FBR tax, EOBI, PF, gratuity)
- No Urdu/Arabic support
- USD pricing — too expensive for local SMBs (PKR 4–7 lakh/month for a 100-person company)
- No local bank integration
- Slow, foreign support

**MatrixHR fills this gap** with a modern, locally-built HR platform priced for our market.

---

### 👥 Target Customers

- Pakistani and GCC SMBs with **20–500 employees**
- Software houses, manufacturers, retail chains, schools, hospitals, agencies
- Initial focus: **Pakistan** → expand to UAE, Saudi, Bangladesh

---

### 🧱 Core Modules

**Phase 1 — MVP (Months 1–6)**

1. **Employee Database** — Central record of all employees, documents, org chart, custom fields
2. **Leave Management** — Customizable policies, approvals, "Who's Out" calendar, Hijri/Gregorian
3. **Attendance & Time Tracking** — Web + mobile clock-in, biometric device integration, geo-fencing
4. **Onboarding** — Workflow templates, document collection, checklists for new hires
5. **WhatsApp Integration** — Apply for leave, approve, get payslips, all via WhatsApp (our killer feature)
6. **Self-Service Portal** — Employees manage their own profile, leave, attendance, documents

**Phase 2 — Months 7–12**

7. **Payroll Engine** — FBR tax slabs, EOBI, PF, gratuity, loans, bonuses, payslip generation
8. **Bank File Generation** — Direct salary disbursement files for Meezan, HBL, UBL, MCB
9. **Recruitment / ATS** — Job posting, candidate pipeline, offer letters with e-signature
10. **Performance Management** — Goals, reviews, 360 feedback, continuous feedback

**Phase 3 — Months 13–18**

11. **AI-Powered Features** — Resume screening, performance summaries, policy assistant
12. **Built-in LMS** — Courses, quizzes, certifications
13. **Marketplace Integrations** — Foodpanda, Careem, telemedicine, insurance, NADRA Verisys
14. **Advanced Analytics** — Custom dashboards, predictive insights

### 💰 Business Model

**Pricing Tiers** (per employee per month, in PKR):
- **Starter** — Core HR, leave, attendance, employee portal
- **Growth** — Adds payroll, recruitment, performance
- **Enterprise** — Adds AI features, LMS, advanced analytics, dedicated support

**Additional Revenue:**
- Premium integrations (priced separately)
- Marketplace commissions (Foodpanda, insurance partners, telemedicine)
- One-time setup/implementation fees
- Custom development for enterprise clients

---

### 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Backend:** Node.js + NestJS + PostgreSQL + Redis
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14 + Tailwind + shadcn/ui
- **Mobile:** React Native (Expo)
- **Build Tool:** AI-assisted development via Antigravity (faster build cycle)
- **Hosting:** DigitalOcean or Hetzner (cost-efficient for our market)

---

### 🎨 Design Standard

- Modern, polished UI on par with Linear, Vercel, Stripe
- Full dark mode and light mode
- English + Urdu + Arabic with RTL support
- Mobile-first responsive
- WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility
